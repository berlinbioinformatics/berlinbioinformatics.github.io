Berlin Bioinformatics Social Meeting

Meet Fellow Bioinformaticians

The Berlin Bioinformatics meeting is a bimonthly, regular meeting for Berlin bioinformaticians, biostatisticians and computational biologists. The meeting usually takes place on the last Tuesday of every second month (starting with January), at 18:00 in Berlin Mitte. This is a small website for the meeting for collecting links and slides.

There is not much to see here, most content lives in the wiki and communication happens on the mailing list.

Please subscribe to the mailing list for announcements of the next meeting.
